About

The Declutter Games is a side project of Cheryl Wu. I'm a 20-year-old New Yorker who lives in a 350-square-foot studio apartment with my boyfriend and a rotating cast of guests. All items in my apartment therefore need to prove their worth, or perish.

I enjoy the Hunger Games films, Jennifer Lawrence, and starting novelty tumblrs, so I made this to motivate myself to declutter.

Every fallen tribute is laid to rest on Freecycle or Craigslist. If you're in NYC, you can pick up anything that interests you! (One person's unnamed tribute from District 8 is another's Peeta Mellark.)
